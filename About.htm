About OAE

Oluwatosin Academy of Excellence (OAE) is a forward-thinking educational platform committed to academic brilliance, digital empowerment, and community growth. Founded in 2023 by Mr. Tee, OAE was created with the goal of redefining education in Nigeria, across Africa, and worldwide, by making learning more accessible, engaging, and impactful for students at all levels.

"Education is not just about passing exams, it's about building futures and empowering minds to shape tomorrow."

At OAE, we are deeply passionate about helping students unlock their full potential. Our approach goes beyond traditional classroom learning, combining structured exam preparation, modern digital skills, and engaging educational content to support students in achieving both academic and personal success.

Expert-Led Learning: Learn from experienced educators and industry professionals who understand the Nigerian education system.
Innovative Technology: Cutting-edge digital tools and interactive platforms that make learning fun and effective.

OAE serves as a trusted guide for students preparing for major examinations such as JAMB, WAEC, and NECO. Through carefully curated resources, past questions, practice tools, and explanatory lessons, we help learners build confidence, improve understanding, and perform better in their examinations.

Our Mission

At Oluwatosin Academy of Excellence (OAE), our mission is to empower learners of all ages to reach their full academic and personal potential. We are dedicated to providing high-quality educational resources, structured guidance, and innovative learning tools that combine traditional academic excellence with modern digital literacy.

Our approach is student-centered: we strive to make learning engaging, accessible, and effective. From exam preparation for JAMB, WAEC, NECO, and Post-UTME, to foundational coding and technology skills, OAE equips learners with the knowledge and confidence to excel both in school and beyond.

We also believe in nurturing curiosity, critical thinking, and problem-solving. By blending education with edutainment, motivational content, and community support through OAE CONNECTS, we create an environment where learners are inspired, supported, and motivated to achieve more than they ever thought possible.

Through this mission, OAE aims to transform the educational landscape in Nigeria, across Africa, and worldwide, preparing students not only to succeed academically but to thrive in a fast-changing, tech-driven world.

In addition to exam preparation, OAE promotes lifelong learning, personal development, and tech literacy. By integrating technology, coding education, and edutainment into our platform, we prepare students not only for exams, but also for a future shaped by digital innovation.

Future-Ready Skills: From coding and digital literacy to entrepreneurship and critical thinking.
Global Perspective: Connecting Nigerian students with international opportunities and global best practices.

What We Offer

At OAE, we provide a well-rounded learning experience designed to support students academically, digitally, and mentally. Our offerings are carefully structured to meet the needs of secondary school students, exam candidates, and university learners.

Exam Resources

We provide access to past questions, verified answers, and detailed explanations for all major Nigerian examinations, including JAMB, WAEC, NECO, and Post-UTME. These resources are designed to help students understand exam patterns, improve accuracy, and build confidence through consistent practice.

Subject Tutorials

OAE offers in-depth and easy-to-follow lessons across a wide range of subjects such as Biology, Physics, English Language, Literature-in-English, IRS, Chemistry, Government, and more. Each tutorial is structured to simplify complex topics and align with official exam syllabuses.

Beginner-Friendly Coding

We introduce learners to coding and digital skills through beginner-friendly tutorials in JavaScript, Python, SQL, and other foundational programming concepts. Our approach focuses on clarity, step-by-step explanations, and practical understanding, making tech accessible even to complete beginners.

Advanced Topics

For university students and advanced learners, OAE simplifies higher-level topics such as multi-level statistics and other complex academic concepts. We break these topics down into understandable lessons that support academic success at the tertiary level.

Edutainment & Motivation

Through OAE MEDIA TV, we combine education with entertainment to keep learning engaging and relatable. Our content includes humor, tech news, motivational clips, and academic inspiration designed to encourage consistency, positivity, and a strong learning mindset.

Who We Serve

At OAE, we provide a well-rounded learning experience designed to support students academically, digitally, and mentally. Our offerings are carefully structured to meet the needs of secondary school students, exam candidates, and university learners.

Secondary School Students

We support students preparing for crucial national examinations like JAMB, WAEC, NECO, and GCE. Our comprehensive resources, practice questions, and expert guidance help students build confidence and achieve their academic goals.

University Students

University undergraduates benefit from our advanced tutorials covering complex topics in mathematics, statistics, and other specialized subjects. We help students excel in their courses and prepare for professional careers.

Tech Beginners

Whether you're interested in web development, data analysis, or digital marketing, our beginner-friendly coding tutorials and tech resources make learning programming accessible and enjoyable for complete newcomers.

Lifelong Learners

For those committed to continuous personal and professional development, we offer a wide range of resources to support ongoing learning journeys, skill enhancement, and intellectual growth throughout life.

OAE Platform Features

OAE offers a carefully designed learning platform with essential tools and resources to support consistent study, skills practice, and exam readiness — accessible via the website and downloadable content for offline use.

Past Questions & Solutions

Practice with a curated collection of past exam questions and verified solutions. Each question includes clear explanations to help learners understand exam patterns and improve accuracy.

Video Tutorials

Engaging study guides presented in video format. These tutorials simplify difficult concepts, demonstrate problem-solving steps, and provide practical exam techniques.

Coding Practice

Hands-on coding exercises and practice challenges in languages such as JavaScript and Python, designed to build practical programming skills from beginner level upward.

Exam Updates

Timely notifications and tips about upcoming exams, registration deadlines, and important changes — so learners always stay informed and prepared.

Offline Learning

Downloadable lessons and resources that allow students to study without a constant internet connection. Ideal for learners with limited or intermittent connectivity.

User-Friendly Design

An intuitive, easy-to-navigate platform that makes finding study materials, practice tests, and tutorials fast and simple — designed for smooth learning on phones, tablets, and desktops.
