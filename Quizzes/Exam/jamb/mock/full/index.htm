180 Questions • 1h 45m • 4 Subjects

What is the Full CBT Mock?

This is a comprehensive simulation of the Joint Admissions and Matriculation Board (JAMB) Unified Tertiary Matriculation Examination (UTME). Experience the exact format, timing, and pressure of the real JAMB CBT exam with questions drawn from all four subjects.

Duration

1 hour 45 minutes (105 minutes total)

Questions

180 multiple choice questions

Subjects

English + 3 other subjects

Format

Computer-Based Testing

Subject Distribution

📝

Use of English

60 questions • 45 minutes
Comprehension, Grammar, Lexis & Structure
🧮

Mathematics

40 questions • 27 minutes
Algebra, Geometry, Calculus, Statistics
⚗️

Physics/Chemistry/Biology

40 questions each • 27 minutes each
Science subject of your choice

Important Instructions

  • Strict Timing: The exam cannot be paused once started. Manage your time wisely across all subjects.
  • Navigation: You can move between questions and subjects during the exam.
  • Auto-Save: Your answers are automatically saved as you progress.
  • Review: You can review and change answers before final submission.
  • Internet: Ensure stable internet connection. Use a computer/laptop for best experience.
