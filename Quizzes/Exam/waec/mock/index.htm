WAEC Mock Exam Tips

Time Management

Allocate time wisely. Spend more time on difficult questions but don't get stuck on any single question.

Stay Focused

Maintain concentration throughout the exam. Take short breaks if needed, but stay within time limits.

Review Answers

If time permits, review your answers. Look for careless mistakes and ensure all questions are answered.

Rest Well

Get adequate sleep before the exam. A well-rested mind performs better and makes fewer mistakes.
